Top 5 Restaurants in Zurich, Switzerland

A curated guide for American families visiting this beautiful Swiss city - Updated April 2025

1. Zeughauskeller

Located in a 15th-century armory, Zeughauskeller is a Zurich institution offering authentic Swiss cuisine in a historic setting. The restaurant features rustic wooden tables, historic artifacts, and a lively atmosphere that's perfect for families.

The menu offers traditional Swiss specialties including various sausages, rösti (Swiss hash browns), and their famous meter-long sausage. The portions are generous, making it ideal for hungry travelers. They also offer a good selection of local beers.

What makes it great for your family: The restaurant has a lively atmosphere with communal tables, making it easy to enjoy conversation. The historic setting provides a genuine Swiss experience, and the menu has plenty of options that will appeal to all ages in your group.

Address: Bahnhofstrasse 28A, 8001 Zurich, Switzerland

Phone: +41 44 220 15 15

Website: zeughauskeller.ch

Price Range: $$-$$$

Tip: This restaurant is very popular, so consider making a reservation or arriving early to avoid waiting.

2. Swiss Chuchi Restaurant

For an authentic Swiss fondue experience, Swiss Chuchi is a must-visit. Located in the heart of Zurich's Old Town, this restaurant specializes in traditional Swiss cheese fondue and raclette, offering a truly Swiss dining experience.

The restaurant has a cozy, rustic interior with traditional Swiss décor. Their menu features nine variations of cheese fondue, including classic, champagne, and tomato versions. They also serve other Swiss specialties like rösti and various meat dishes.

What makes it great for your family: Fondue is an interactive dining experience that's perfect for family bonding. The restaurant has a warm, welcoming atmosphere and the staff is known for being friendly and helpful to tourists. It's a fun way to experience Swiss culture through food.

Address: Rosengasse 10, 8001 Zurich, Switzerland

Phone: +41 44 266 96 66

Website: hotel-adler.ch/en/swiss-chuchi-restaurant

Price Range: $$-$$$

Tip: Try the classic cheese fondue with kirsch (cherry brandy) for an authentic Swiss experience.

3. Haus Hiltl

Haus Hiltl holds the Guinness World Record as the oldest vegetarian restaurant in the world, operating since 1898. This elegant establishment offers a vast buffet of over 100 vegetarian and vegan dishes from around the world, making it perfect for families with varied tastes.

The restaurant features a modern, airy interior with a sophisticated ambiance. Their pay-by-weight buffet includes Swiss, Indian, Asian, and Mediterranean dishes. They also offer à la carte options and a cocktail bar with creative non-alcoholic options.

What makes it great for your family: The buffet format allows everyone to choose exactly what they want, making it ideal for different preferences. The restaurant has a comfortable, upscale atmosphere that's still relaxed enough for families. It's a great option for health-conscious diners or those looking for a break from heavy Swiss cuisine.

Address: Sihlstrasse 28, 8001 Zurich, Switzerland

Phone: +41 44 227 70 00

Website: hiltl.ch

Price Range: $$-$$$

Tip: Visit during off-peak hours to avoid crowds, as this is one of Zurich's most popular restaurants.

4. Fork & Bottle

Fork & Bottle is an American-owned restaurant offering a taste of home with a Swiss twist. Located in a beautiful garden setting, this restaurant serves American-style comfort food using locally sourced ingredients.

The restaurant features a large garden with outdoor seating, a playground for children, and a cozy indoor dining area. Their menu includes gourmet burgers, ribs, craft beers, and an excellent wine selection. They're known for their weekend brunches and barbecue.

What makes it great for your family: The spacious garden setting is perfect for relaxing, and the American-inspired menu offers familiar flavors that might be welcome after days of trying new cuisines. The restaurant has a laid-back atmosphere and is particularly popular with expats and English-speaking visitors.

Address: Allmendstrasse 20, 8002 Zurich, Switzerland

Phone: +41 44 201 18 17

Website: forkandbottle.ch

Price Range: $$-$$$

Tip: Their Sunday brunch is extremely popular, so reservations are essential.

5. Tibits

Tibits is a modern vegetarian and vegan restaurant with a relaxed atmosphere and innovative pay-by-weight buffet concept. With multiple locations in Zurich, it's a convenient option no matter where you're staying in the city.

The restaurant offers a fresh, constantly changing buffet with over 40 homemade dishes inspired by cuisines from around the world. The interior is stylish and contemporary, with comfortable seating arrangements suitable for groups.

What makes it great for your family: The casual atmosphere and self-service concept make dining stress-free. The wide variety of dishes ensures everyone can find something they enjoy, and the pay-by-weight system allows for flexibility in portion sizes. It's also a great option for quick, healthy meals between sightseeing activities.

Address: Multiple locations in Zurich (Seefeld, Oerlikon, and near the main train station)

Phone: Varies by location

Website: tibits.ch

Price Range: $$

Tip: The restaurant can get busy during lunch hours, so consider visiting for an early or late lunch to avoid crowds.
